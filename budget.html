Statement Folders
All folders live inside BudgetApp/Statements/. Parser auto-detects formats — no renaming needed.
💳Credit /statements_parser Accepts: .pdf  .csv
Any credit card — Apple, Chase, Citi, Amex, etc.
Apple Card: Wallet app → tap card → ↑ Export icon (top-right) → CSV
Chase / Citi / Amex: Log in → Account Activity → Download → CSV or PDF
🏦Debit /statements_parser Accepts: .csv  .pdf
Any checking or debit account — Fidelity, Chase, BofA, etc.
Fidelity: fidelity.com → Accounts & Trade → your account → Activity & Orders → Download → CSV
⚑ CSV preferred over PDF for Fidelity checking
📈Investments /investments_parser Accepts: Portfolio_Positions_*.csv
Fidelity portfolio positions snapshot.
Fidelity: fidelity.com → Accounts & Trade → Portfolio → Positions → Download CSV
Keep the default filename — the date embedded in it tracks snapshot age.
